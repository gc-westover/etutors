The future of education in the traditional classroom setting is uncertain at the moment. The majority of schools in the United States will be transitioning to the virtual classroom in some form during the upcoming school year. Some students may struggle with online learning, that's where we come in! Welcome to the Eureka Center, our virtual learning classroom. The eTutors are here to help students by implementing learning objectives that improve self-sufficiency with online learning, as well as promote enjoyment and success! The Eureka Center is constantly adapting to the online learning environment by introducing many diverse tools for learning such as Upswing, Moodle, and Buncee. We also have upcoming webinars and workshops to help families become more acquainted with these technologies. Academic coaches are available for one-on-one meetings, or can be reached on Zoom Monday through Friday 10 a.m. - 3 p.m. Read more about our services here
